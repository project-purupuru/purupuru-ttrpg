## Implication

The hackathon scaffold has no env-var dependencies in `app/` or `lib/`. There is no `.env`, `.env.local`, or `.env.example` in the repo. `.gitignore` includes `.env*` patterns but no env files are tracked or referenced.

[INFERRED] When real backends are wired (Score API, on-chain RPC, IRL weather), env vars will become necessary. None exist today.

## Notes

- `next/font/google` (Inter, Geist Mono) makes outbound network calls at build time but does not require env config.
- Pixi.js, motion, lucide-react, clsx, tailwind-merge are all client-side and env-free.
